About

Welcome to my personal website. I'm a passionate writer, thinker, and technology enthusiast with a keen interest in exploring the intersections of culture, philosophy, and innovation.

Background

With a background in Computer Science and Philosophy, I bring a unique perspective to my work. My essays reflect my interdisciplinary approach, often bridging the gap between technology and humanities.

Professional Experience

I have over a decade of experience in the tech industry, working with startups and established companies alike. My roles have ranged from software development to product management, giving me a comprehensive understanding of the digital landscape.

Writing and Research

My writing focuses on the impact of technology on society, the philosophy of everyday life, and the future of work. I regularly contribute to tech blogs and philosophy journals, and I'm currently working on a book about digital ethics.
